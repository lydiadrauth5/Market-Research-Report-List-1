<p><p>The High Resolution Inspection System market encompasses various technologies, primarily Optical Inspection Systems and X-ray Inspection Systems. Optical Inspection Systems utilize cameras and sensors to detect surface defects and ensure quality in manufacturing processes, often employing techniques like machine vision. In contrast, X-ray Inspection Systems provide internal examination of products, revealing hidden flaws and material consistencies without damaging the samples. Both systems play critical roles in ensuring product reliability across industries, particularly in electronics, automotive, and medical sectors.</p></p>
